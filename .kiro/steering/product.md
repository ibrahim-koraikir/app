# Product Overview

Entertainment Browser is an Android application that provides unified access to 45+ entertainment websites across streaming services, TV shows, books, and video platforms. Users can browse content, manage tabs, download videos from compatible sites, and organize their entertainment experience through favorites and sessions.

## Key Features

- Browse 45+ entertainment websites organized by category (Streaming, TV Shows, Books, Video Platforms)
- In-app WebView browsing with tab management (up to 20 tabs)
- Video detection and download capabilities for compatible sites
- Favorites management for quick access to preferred websites
- Session management to save and restore collections of tabs
- Search functionality across all websites
- Onboarding flow for first-time users
- Dark theme with Material Design 3

## Target Platform

- Android API 24+ (Android 7.0 Nougat and above)
- Target SDK: 36
- Minimum SDK: 24
